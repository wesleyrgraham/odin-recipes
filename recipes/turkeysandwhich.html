Turkey Sammich

Almost a hearty meal!

Not for vegetarians or vegans

Tons of protein in every bite!

No dishes to clean afterwards!

Ingredients and Suggested Materials

  • 1 paper towl (to be used as a plate and to wipe your mouth later)
  • 2 pieces of deli turkey
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 2 slices of bread

Assembly Instructions

  1. Place the two pieces of bread on the paper towl
  2. Place the two slices of turkey on one peice of bread
  3. Place the two slice of cheese on top of the turkey
  4. Place the empty piece of bread on the cheese
  5. Enjoy!

Poverty Kitchen NEXT LEVEL Techniques

  • Putting potato chips on top of the cheese covers up the taste of cheap turkey better than putting them under the turkey
  • Putting a little butter on the bread and frying it in a pan turns this recipe into a turkey grilled cheese!
